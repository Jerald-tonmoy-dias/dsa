📌 6-Month DSA Learning Plan

⏳ Daily Commitment: 1 Hour

📆 Duration: 6 Months (~24 Weeks)

📍 Phase 1: Fundamentals & Core Patterns (Weeks 1-6)

🔹 Week 1: Arrays & Hashing (Frequency Counter)

📖 Concepts to Learn:

  • Arrays Basics
  • Hash Tables & Hash Maps
  • Frequency Counter Pattern

📝 Problems:

  • Two Sum
  • Valid Anagram
  • Contains Duplicate
  • Group Anagrams
  • Longest Consecutive Sequence

📚 Learning Resources:

  • NeetCode - Arrays & Hashing (YouTube)
  • Leetcode Arrays Guide
  • GeeksforGeeks Hashing Guide

🔹 Week 2: Two Pointers & Sliding Window

📖 Concepts to Learn:

  • Two Pointer Technique
  • Sliding Window Approach

📝 Problems:

  • Two Pointers:
    • Valid Palindrome
    • Two Sum II (Sorted Array)
    • 3Sum
    • Container With Most Water
  • Sliding Window:
    • Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock
    • Longest Substring Without Repeating Characters
    • Maximum Sum Subarray of Size K

📚 Learning Resources:

  • NeetCode - Two Pointers (YouTube)
  • Sliding Window Pattern - CodeWithMosh (YouTube)

🔹 Week 3: Stack, Queue & Recursion

📖 Concepts to Learn:

  • Stack & Queue Basics
  • Recursion Fundamentals

📝 Problems:

  • Stack & Queue:
    • Valid Parentheses
    • Min Stack
    • Daily Temperatures
    • Next Greater Element I
  • Recursion:
    • Fibonacci Number
    • Power of Two

📚 Learning Resources:

  • NeetCode - Stacks & Queues (YouTube)
  • GeeksforGeeks - Stack Data Structure
